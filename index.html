1. Problem → Solution

Every year, thousands of children go missing or face potentially dangerous situations. Current solutions (GPS watches, bulky trackers) are not discreet, easy to remove, or smart enough to detect danger before it escalates.

SmartButton.AI is a hidden, ultra‑small wearable device for kids that provides:

  • Real‑time GPS tracking
  • Instant SOS alerts
  • AI‑based danger detection (movement anomalies, geofence violations, panic patterns)
  • Secure mobile app for parents
